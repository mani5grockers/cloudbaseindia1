'''CloudBaseIndia''' is an Indian web hosting and digital services provider based in Gummidipoondi, Tamil Nadu. The company provides shared hosting, WordPress hosting, e-commerce hosting, cloud hosting, VPS hosting, and domain reseller services, along with other digital paid services.<ref name="cloudbase_official">{{cite web |title=CloudBaseIndia – Official Website |url=https://cloudbaseindia.com |website=CloudBaseIndia |access-date=25 January 2026}}</ref>

== History ==
CloudBaseIndia was founded in {{Start date and age|2026}} by '''Manikandan M'''.<ref name="cloudbase_official" />
It offers hosting and infrastructure services primarily to individuals, startups, and small businesses in India.<ref name="cloudbase_official" />

== Products and services ==
CloudBaseIndia provides hosting and digital services such as:

=== Web hosting ===
* Shared hosting (cPanel)<ref name="cloudbase_official" />
* WordPress hosting<ref name="cloudbase_official" />
* E-commerce hosting<ref name="cloudbase_official" />

=== Cloud and server hosting ===
* Cloud hosting plans<ref name="cloudbase_official" />

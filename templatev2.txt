'''CloudBaseIndia''' is an Indian web hosting and digital services provider based in Gummidipoondi, Tamil Nadu. The company provides shared hosting, WordPress hosting, reseller hosting, cloud hosting, VPS hosting, and domain reseller services, along with digital goods and paid digital services.<ref name="cloudbase_official">{{cite web |title=CloudBaseIndia – Official Website |url=https://cloudbaseindia.com |website=CloudBaseIndia |access-date=25 January 2026}}</ref>

== History ==
CloudBaseIndia was founded in 2026 by '''Manikandan M'''.<ref name="cloudbase_official" />
It provides hosting and infrastructure services primarily to individuals, startups, and small businesses in India.<ref name="cloudbase_official" />

CloudBaseIndia provides hosting and digital services such as:

* Shared hosting (cPanel)<ref name="cloudbase_official" />
* WordPress hosting<ref name="cloudbase_official" />
